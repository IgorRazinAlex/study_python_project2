A quick Fan-Manga introduction

What are we?

Konichiwa, fellow manga fan!

We`re happy to see you on our fan-manga site! This site gives you access to thousands of fan mangas, manhwas, ranobes and others. Here`s a little instruction about what can you do on our site.

Account management

You can create your own account with a unique name, where you can see all your comments that you`ve made

Manga stuff

Reading

Of course you can read manga! Just click "Read" on manga`s page and you`re off to go! You can choose filters for your manga page. Sorting by popularity and update date are only available options at the moment

Commenting

You can comment different mangas and give them a star rating from 1 to 5.*

Posting

You can post your mangas.* We ask you not to post other people`s mangas without their permission. If you`re an author and see your work posted like that, contact us at fanmangahelp@gmail.com for more details

Changing

You can change info about your manga or preview to it. Currently you can change preview to your manga only one time

Adding chapters

You can add chapters to your manga. Remember, that you need to choose them in correct order for them to upload properly

*Only possible for registered users
